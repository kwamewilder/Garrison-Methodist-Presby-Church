Find Us

Located in the heart of Garrison, we're easy to find and there's plenty of parking

By Car

Free parking available in our lot behind the church. Additional street parking on Church Street.

By Bus

Take A Burma Camp bus from Circle, Accra or 37 and alight at Supply or Air Force Bus stop. We're just a 3-minute walk from the station.

Accessibility

Our building is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps and accessible restrooms.
